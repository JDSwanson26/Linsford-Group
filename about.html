About Linsford Group

25+ Years of Excellence in Global Freight Forwarding

Our Story

Founded in 2001, Linsford Group has grown to become one of the leading freight forwarding companies in the industry. What started as a small operation has evolved into a global logistics powerhouse serving clients across more than 150 countries.

Our commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction has driven every decision we've made. We understand that in the freight forwarding industry, every shipment matters, and every deadline is crucial.

Today, Linsford Group operates a network of offices and partnerships worldwide, employing over 500 logistics professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional service.
