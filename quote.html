Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Quoting Process

How long does it take to get a quote?

Most quotes are provided within 2-4 hours for standard shipments. Complex shipments may take up to 24 hours.

What information do you need?

We need details about your cargo (weight, dimensions), origin, destination, and preferred shipping method.

Is the quote binding?

Our quotes are valid for 30 days and provide guidance on pricing. Final rates depend on market conditions and exact specifications.

Are all fees included?

Yes, our quotes include all applicable charges: freight, handling, taxes, and any selected services. No hidden fees.

Can I get emergency shipping?

Yes! We offer express and rush services. Contact our team directly for emergency shipping requirements.

What if my shipment is hazardous?

We handle hazmat shipments with full compliance. Provide details in the "Special Requirements" section of the form.
